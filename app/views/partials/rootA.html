melissa

She's done commercial work for companies large and small, and she teaches classes regularly in the Salt Lake City, Utah area. Melissa publishes her work at MelissaEsplin.com (aka I Still Love You, aka ISLY), her craft/design/everything blog.

I Still Love Calligraphy is a joint production of halves one and two of the Esplin duo. Chris does the web development and Melissa creates everything else. If there's a problem, it's probably Chris's fault, and you should drop us an email. Chris will get right on it.
