You will learn to letter beautiful envelopes, invitations, save-the-dates, love letters and scrapbook layouts. Melissa's students have used calligraphy to supplement their design, photography and event planning businesses.

There is no easy way to learn calligraphy. It requires consistent practice like every other valuable skill. However, calligraphy is not nearly as difficult as it first appears. Melissa has personally taught over 3,000 students how to letter since 2009, and her online teaching methodology has proven to be an efficient way for students to start lettering immediately.

The course starts with an overview of the supplies that you will need to get started. Then you'll use Melissa's custom practice guides to trace the letterforms with a pencil. From the pencil, you progress to pointed pen work, tracing letterforms and eventually forming words and positioning those words on the page.

The class's secret sauce is Melissa's totally unique feedback gallery. As you complete your assignments, you will upload snapshots of your work to your personal feedback gallery. You can use the gallery to ask Melissa any questions that you have. Melissa will review your work, answer your questions and give you suggestions for improvement. This form of one-on-one coaching will accelerate your learning far faster than reading and practice guides alone.
