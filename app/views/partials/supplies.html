Get it Locally.

Sourcing locally can be time consuming and frustrating unless you live in a relatively urban area or an artsy college town. If you do, you may just be able to find everything you need in one stop.

To expedite your shopping trip, print out this page (or save a tree and pull it up on your phone) and have your local art supply store employee help you find everything you need.

Purchase Supplies

Required Supplies:

  • Wooden or plastic pen holder
  • Black ink - FW, Higgins Eternal, Sumi 60
  • At least 2 Nibs - Hunt 22, Hunt 101, Gillott 303, Brause ef66, Leonardt Principal, Zebra G
  • Organizer box
  • Rhodia gridor Bienfang grid paper
  • Tracing paper
  • Pencil
  • White plastic eraser

Additional Supplies:

  • Oblique pen holder (strongly recommended for lefties)
  • Sumi 60 ink
  • Dr. PH Martin’s Pen White ink
  • Dr. PH Martin’s Copperplate Gold
  • All the pointed nibs you can find - Hunt 22, Hunt 101, Gillott 303, Brause ef66, Leonardt Principal, Zebra G
  • Drafting pencil
  • Crowquill holder
  • Click eraser
  • Clear gridded ruler
