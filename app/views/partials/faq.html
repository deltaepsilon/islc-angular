Frequently Asked Questions


If you can’t find a solution in the FAQ’s, please email us with your question.

IS THE WORKSHOP JUST FOR BEGINNERS?

Yes and no. We start from the very beginning, no prior knowledge necessary.

If you’re signing up with prior knowledge and skill, be sure to mention in the comments of your gallery posts. I’m happy to give you a challenge with more off-script assignments.

IS THE WORKSHOP FOR LEFTIES?

Pointed pen calligraphy is great for both lefties and righties. You’ll want to make sure that you order both straight and oblique holders (universal oblique by speedball in the Optional Items supply page) and read this article about proper hand positioning for lefties.

WHEN DOES MY SUBSCRIPTION START?

Your subscriptions starts when you first access the coursework via the Subscriptions page. The “Time Remaining” column will start the countdown.

CAN I COMPLETE THE COURSEWORK IN ONLY 30 DAYS?

  • Spend 2-4 hours per week going through coursework and practicing. 15-20 minutes per night is all it takes.

  • Make sure that you complete the assignment prompts and upload to the gallery. No fancy scans needed. A simple phone pic will do just fine.

  • Upload images of your calligraphy problems to the gallery. You have an expert in your corner for 30 days to help you figure out any little hiccup you come across.

DO YOU OFFER EXTENSIONS FOR THE SUBSCRIPTION?

We don't offer extensions at this time, although you can purchase the class again and the extra time will be added to your existing subscription. We made sure that the subscription was the right amount of time for our beta testers and they all agreed that 30 days was just right. If you do need an extension, feel free to drop us a line at support@melissaesplin.com

CAN I PURCHASE MY SUPPLIES BEFORE MY SUBSCRIPTION? WHAT DO I BUY?

We've been working with Paper & Ink Arts to make getting started as easy as possible. Head over to their conferences and classes section and select the I Still Love Calligraphy course. There you'll be able to find all of the basics you'll need and a few optional items to get started.

Read more about the supplies and how to source them locally here.

IS THE COURSE ON AN INDIVIDUAL BASIS?

Yes, it is! That's the beauty of it. You can access the course at any time and work at your own pace. Feedback is on an individual basis as well.

Through feedback we can re-work concepts that you’re struggling with or go off-script to more advanced items. Work completely at your own pace.

IS THE UPLOAD GALLERY VIEWABLE BY EVERYONE?

No, it is not. The galleries are all private between the user and admin. Students and alumni have been posting their work to the I Still Love Calligraphy Facebook page.

WHEN DO YOU LEAVE FEEDBACK?

I check in on Mondays and Fridays weekly starting at 9am MST. Every once in a while life can get the best of me (vacations, illness, holidays, etc) and I can’t get to feedback that day. It’s not often, but when it happens I have systems in place to add an extra day or two (as applicable) to all active (or recently active) subscriptions.

WHAT IF I NEED FEEDBACK ASAP?

If you need urgent feedback before you start the next assignment, upload your work to the gallery and shoot me a short email with your username.

Giving great feedback when you need it is my number 1 priority. No feedback is given on Sundays.

CAN I SUBMIT MY ASSIGNMENTS VIA EMAIL?

No. Technically you can, but my time and the gallery is structured so that no assignment uploaded into the gallery gets forgotten. I give feedback on Mondays and Fridays, so if you email me your assignment when I’m not working on feedback, it will likely get lost in my inbox black hole. For best results, upload your work to the gallery and email me if feedback is needed urgently. I’ll try to be as prompt as I can.

IS THIS WEBSITE IPAD COMPATIBLE?

It had better be. We’ve put in a lot of hours testing the course on iPad and iPhone using iOS7. You can even upload images to the student gallery directly your from your iOS device’s camera roll. A tablet like the iPad is the easiest way to take the course for many users.

I DON’T HAVE A SCANNER, IS THAT A PROBLEM?

No scanner, no problem. In fact, sometimes a regular phone/tablet photo will give me more insight for feedback than a flat scan. Try to take photos in as much light as possible. Turning on extra lamps and lights should be sufficient for taking photos at night.

WILL COURSE MATERIALS BE AVAILABLE TO STUDENTS EVEN AFTER THE 30 DAYS?

The course includes printable guides for practicing. The guides are yours to keep and use as you work on polishing your new skill.

The web course has a nifty advantage over her face-to-face classes, because you get coursework, video instruction, practice materials, and Melissa's personal attention for a whole month. Work your way through each lesson, complete the assignments, and upload your work for Melissa to critique.

HOW CAN I MAXIMIZE MY VIDEO QUALITY?

Different web browsers use different video formats. Our videos are natively in H.264 format for excellent compatibility on all Apple products and newer browser. They have also been transcoded to the WebM format for compatibility with older browsers.

See this H.264 compatibilty chart for details.

SOME ELEMENT ON THE SITE LOOKS WONKY.

We've done our web development using the latest Chrome, Safari and Firefox browsers. Sorry IE folks. We've tried to make everything compatible, but we just don't have the time/resources to test in IE. Upgrade your browser to the newest version of IE (IE10 or IE11), and if that doesn't fix it, switch to Chrome, Firefox or Safari. If you're really frustrated, email us some feedback.
